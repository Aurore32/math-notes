Theorem. The set of all (real) n×nn\times nn×n length-preserving transformation matrices form a group.

Proof. By the previous theorem, real length-preserving transformation matrices are orthogonal matrices and vice versa. As orthogonal matrices form a group, so must length-preserving transformation matrices.
