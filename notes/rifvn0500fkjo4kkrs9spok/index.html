Non-Linear Equations

This section studies any general equation of the form

Definition. (Separable equations). Definition. Simply-connected domain. A domain D\mathbf{D}D is simply-connected if:

  1. The domain is connected (closed) and not open.
  2. Any closed loop in D\mathbf{D}D can be shrunk to a point that is still on D\mathbf{D}D.

For instance, a sphere is simply-connected while a donut is not. Yum!
