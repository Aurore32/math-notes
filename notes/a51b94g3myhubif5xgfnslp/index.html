Limits

With truly astounding mathematical insight and ingenuity, we define the vector or scalar l\mathbf{l}l in

lim⁡x→af(x)=l\lim_{\mathbf{x\to a}}f(\mathbf{x}) = \mathbf{l}x→alim​f(x)=l
